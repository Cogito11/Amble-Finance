FEATURES

Everything you need. Nothing you don't.

Amble covers the basics of managing money well, without becoming complicated.

Accounts, all in one place

Track checking, savings, and credit cards together, with your net worth calculated automatically as you go.

Budgets you can actually see

Set a limit for any category and watch a live gauge show what's spent and what's left. No math required.

Plans that repeat themselves

Build a budget around a paycheck, split it into categories, and set it active. Have it repeat weekly, every two weeks, monthly, or on your own schedule.

Transactions in seconds

Log income, expenses, and transfers quickly, then search and filter your history whenever you need it.

A dashboard that tells you something

See net worth, spending by category, and income versus expenses over the last six months at a glance.

Your numbers stay yours

Everything is stored on your own computer. No account to create, no server to trust, nothing to subscribe to.

GETTING STARTED

Up and running in three steps.

01

Add your accounts
